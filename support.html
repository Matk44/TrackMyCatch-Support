Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate is the Fish AI identification feature?

Our AI-powered identification system uses advanced image recognition trained on thousands of fish species. Accuracy is highest in good lighting conditions and clear photos. For confirmation, we recommend cross-referencing with local fishing guides or regulations.

Q: Is my GPS location data secure and private?

Yes. Your GPS coordinates and location data are encrypted and only used locally on your device or shared with the global map only when you choose to share a catch. Your privacy is our priority. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Q: Can I export my catch log?

You can view and screenshot your catch history within the app. For bulk export requests, contact our support team at the email address provided above.

Q: Why isn't the fish species ID recognizing my photo?

The AI works best with clear, well-lit photos showing the fish's distinctive features. Try photographing the fish from different angles in bright daylight. If issues persist, email us with your photo and we'll investigate.

Q: Does the app work offline?

Core features like catch logging work offline and sync automatically when connectivity is restored. GPS mapping and community features require an internet connection.

Q: How do I delete my account and data?

To request account deletion or data removal, email us at support@trackmycatch.com with your request. We will process deletions within 30 days as required by privacy laws.

Q: Are the fishing regulations accurate?

Our AI-generated regulation information is provided as a reference guide based on your GPS location. Always verify current regulations with official local fishing authorities before fishing. We are not responsible for regulation changes or inaccuracies.

Q: How often is the fish species database updated?

Our database is updated regularly with new species and improved identification algorithms. Updates are delivered automatically via app updates.
